1. Executive Summary

The TRK Blockchain Real Cash Game Ecosystem is a decentralized gaming and rewards platform built on Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). It integrates real-cash gaming, referral systems, cashback protection, and automated income mechanisms through smart contracts.
